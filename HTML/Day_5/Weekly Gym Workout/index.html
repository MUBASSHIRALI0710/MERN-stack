Weekly Gym Workout Plan

  • Monday Workout Plan (Points) - Chest & Triceps Focus
  • Tuesday: Back + Biceps
  • Wednesday: Legs + Abs
  • Thursday: Legs (Quads + Hamstrings + Calves)
  • Friday: Legs
  • Saturday: Shoulders + Traps

A balanced 6-day workout plan targets different muscle groups each day for overall strength and growth. Start Monday with Chest, focusing on presses and fly movements. Tuesday hits Back with rows, pull-downs, and deadlifts. Wednesday is for Shoulders, including overhead presses and lateral raises. Thursday focuses on Arms, training biceps and triceps with curls and dips. Friday is for Legs, emphasizing squats, lunges, and leg presses. Saturday ends with Core & Functional Training, including planks, crunches, and stability exercises. This split ensures proper recovery while building strength and muscle evenly across the week.

Monday Workout Plan - Chest & Triceps Focus

🗓️ Monday: Chest + Triceps

  • Bench Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Cable Fly – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Push-Ups – 2 sets × max reps
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Tricep Rope Pushdown – 3 sets × 12 reps

✨ Benefits:

  • Builds strong and defined chest muscles
  • Improves pushing strength for daily activities
  • Enhances upper-body stability and aesthetics
  • Strengthens triceps for compound lifts

✔ Advantages:

  • Great for overall upper-body power
  • Helps improve posture and physique
  • Boosts strength for bench and push movements

✖ Disadvantages:

  • Heavy bench press may stress shoulders
  • Overtraining triceps can affect next-day pull workouts
  • Poor form can reduce chest activation

💬 Daily Quote:

"Start your week with strength—your chest builds the confidence to lift more than just weights."

Tuesday Workout Plan - Back + Biceps

Workout Plan:

  • Pull-Ups - 3 sets x 8-10 reps
  • Lat Pulldown - 4 sets x 10-12 reps
  • Seated Cable Row - 3 sets x 12 reps
  • Deadlift - 3 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Deadlift - 3 sets x 6-8 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curl - 3 sets x 10 reps
  • Hammer Curls - 3 sets x 12 reps

✨ Benefits

  • Improves posture and spinal strength
  • Builds V-shape back and stronger arms
  • Enhances pulling power for other exercises
  • Boosts grip strength

✔ Advantages

  • Helps increase overall body strength
  • Great for muscle definition
  • Balances upper-body development

✖ Disadvantages

  • Deadlifts require correct form (injury risk if wrong)
  • Grip fatigue may reduce performance
  • Overtraining biceps can limit weekly recovery

💬 Daily Quote

  • "Your back may be behind you, but building it puts your future ahead of you."

🗓️ Wednesday: Legs + Abs

Workout Plan (Points):

  • Squats – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Lunges – 3 sets × 10 reps (each leg)
  • Leg Curl – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Calf Raises – 4 sets × 15–20 reps
  • Plank – 3 sets × 45–60 seconds
  • Leg Raises – 3 sets × 12–15 reps

✨ Benefits:

  • Builds lower-body strength and stability
  • Boosts athletic performance and endurance
  • Improves balance, mobility, and posture
  • Enhances core stability and reduces injury risk

✔ Advantages:

  • Helps increase testosterone and overall muscle growth
  • Improves body symmetry and full-body strength
  • Burns high calories due to big muscle activation

✖ Disadvantages:

  • Leg days can be physically exhausting
  • DOMS (soreness) may last longer than upper-body days
  • Incorrect squatting form may stress knees

💬 Daily Quote:

"Legs carry your body, but discipline carries your dreams."

🗓️ Thursday: Legs (Quads + Hamstrings + Calves)

  • Squats – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 4 sets × 12 reps
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3 sets × 10 reps
  • Leg Extension – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • Leg Curl – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Standing Calf Raise – 4 sets × 15–20 reps

✨ Benefits:

  • Builds powerful lower body strength
  • Improves posture, stability, and balance
  • Enhances athletic performance
  • Boosts testosterone and growth hormone naturally

✔ Advantages:

  • Strengthens the entire lower body
  • Burns high calories due to large muscle involvement
  • Improves overall body mobility

✖ Disadvantages:

  • Heavy squats can strain knees if done with poor form
  • DOMS (muscle soreness) can be intense for beginners
  • Requires proper warm-up to avoid hamstring injury

💬 Daily Quote:

“Strong legs build a strong foundation — for your body and your life.”

🗓️ Friday: Legs

Workout Plan (Points):

  • Squats – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Romanian Deadlift – 3 sets × 10 reps
  • Lunges – 3 sets × 12 reps (each leg)
  • Leg Curl – 3 sets × 15 reps
  • Standing Calf Raises – 4 sets × 15–20 reps

✨ Benefits:

  • Builds stronger and thicker legs
  • Improves overall athletic performance
  • Enhances balance, stability, and lower-body mobility
  • Boosts testosterone and growth hormone levels naturally

✔ Advantages:

  • Helps burn more calories due to large muscles involved
  • Strengthens knees, hips, and lower back
  • Improves power for running and sports

✖ Disadvantages:

  • Leg day can be very tiring and demanding
  • Incorrect squat form may strain the knees or lower back
  • Extended soreness (DOMS) can affect next-day activities

💬 Daily Quote:

"Legs carry your body, but discipline carries your dreams."

🗓️ Saturday: Shoulders + Traps

  • Overhead Barbell Press – 4 sets × 8–10 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Front Raises – 3 sets × 12 reps
  • Reverse Pec Deck / Rear Delt Fly – 3 sets × 12–15 reps
  • Barbell Shrugs – 4 sets × 10–12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets × 15 reps

✨ Benefits

  • Builds broader and stronger shoulders
  • Improves upper-body symmetry
  • Enhances posture and neck stability
  • Boosts overall strength for push exercises

✔ Advantages

  • Great for aesthetics (wider frame look)
  • Strengthens shoulder mobility and stability
  • Helps prevent shoulder injuries

✖ Disadvantages

  • Overhead press can strain shoulders if form breaks
  • Too much shrugging can cause neck tightness
  • Requires proper warm-up to avoid rotator cuff issues

💬 Daily Quote

"Strong shoulders don't just lift weight; they lift confidence."
