Discover Kolkata

"To understand the heart and soul of Kolkata, one must wander its vibrant streets and immerse oneself in its rich tapestry of culture and history."
- Anonymous As the sun dips below the horizon, casting a warm glow over the bustling streets of Kolkata, you will find yourself swept away by the city's magnetic charm. From its iconic landmarks to its bustling markets and mouthwatering street food, Kolkata is a treasure trove waiting to be discovered. Join us on a journey through the City of Joy as we unravel its many secrets and immerse ourselves in its vibrant spirit. Despite its bustling streets and chaotic traffic, Kolkata exudes a unique charm and warmth that captivates visitors.

Top Activities in Kolkata

  • Explore Victoria Memorial & Maidan
  • Walk Across Howrah Bridge & Visit the Ghats
  • Shop and Eat at New Market or Park Street
  • Discover Art & Culture in Kumartuli and College Street
  • Experience Spiritual Peace at Dakshineswar & Belur Math

How To Plan Your Trip

  1. Set Your Duration & Must-Visits – Decide how many days you have (3–5 days ideal) and shortlist main attractions like Victoria Memorial, Howrah Bridge, and Dakshineswar.
  2. Plan Stay & Transport – Book a hotel near Park Street or Esplanade for easy access; use Metro, yellow taxis, or app cabs to get around.
  3. Add Food & Culture Stops – Include local food spots (Flurys, Peter Cat, Tiretti Bazaar breakfast) and cultural walks (College Street, Kumartuli).
